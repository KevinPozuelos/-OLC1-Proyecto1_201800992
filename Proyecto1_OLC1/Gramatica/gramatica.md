Expresiones Regulares
-------

Letras: <code>[a-zA-Z_]+ </code>, <code>[a-zA-Z_]</code> \n
Digitos: <code>[0-9]+ </code>, <code> [0-9] </code>\n
Decimales: <code> [0-9]+("."[  |0-9]+)? </code>\n
Signos: <code>[!\"\'%+"|"¿¡#$&.+*_] </code>, <code>[!-"}"\040] </code>\n
